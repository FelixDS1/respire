Go / No-Go Decision

Three outcomes. Honest criteria. Decided on the numbers alone.

Continue + scale

60+ active therapists · 150+ sessions in July · MoM session growth ≥30% in June & July · 5+ paying premium therapists · Therapist churn <20%

Continue bootstrapped

30–60 therapists · 50–150 sessions/month · Growing but slowly · Extend runway, don't raise yet · Reassess in 3 months

Pause

<30 therapists despite full outbound effort · Demo→signup <15% · Patient retention <20% · No clear path to growth
